We are SponsorPortalen

SponsorPortalen is an innovative platform for collaborations between sponsors and sports associations/teams. We specialize in mediating the contact between the parties and ensure that all administrative handling is handled efficiently and smoothly. This includes contract writing, invoicing, payment to the association or team, feedback, as well as a variety of related services that are important to create a successful collaboration.

We have a strong policy of openness and transparency and are honest and clear about our business. We charge a fee for our services, but for us Sponsorportalen is more than just a business - it is a passion for simplifying collaborations between sponsors and sports associations/teams. We recommend interested parties to explore our website to get a more detailed picture of our business and our values.

Here's How It Works Practically

Phase 1

Step 1

Select County

Select the geographical area where you wish to support an association with sponsorship.

Step 2

Select Sports

Choose the sport you wish to support.

Step 3

Select Gender

Choose whether you wish to focus your support on boys, girls or if it is unimportant to you.

Step 4

Select Age Group

Select the desired age group for the sports group you wish to support with our sponsorship services or select if it is unimportant.

Step 5

Select Package

Choose the sponsorship package that enables your desired involvement and support for the sports group, choose between our fixed packages (bronze, silver or gold) or a flexible solution adapted to your wishes.

Step 6

Complete Your Application

Complete your application by submitting your and company details. Please note that this is the first step in the process and no invoice will be sent until you approve the sponsorship.

Phase 2

Step 1

Initial Contact With the Association

We contact associations based on your wishes. If no specific team is selected, we will select a suitable association from our database.

Step 2

Anchor Sponsorship

We anchor your sponsorship by ensuring the association's acceptance and willingness to complete the agreement based on the chosen sponsorship package.

Step 3

Feedback

We will notify you of the match between your wishes and a suitable team, as well as send information about this group. We verify your satisfaction and approval to complete the agreement.

Step 4

Customer reasoning

In the event of a no, we carry out further preference surveys and continue searching until we have found a choice that you are satisfied with.

Step 5

Customer satisfaction

If yes, we generate a formal sponsorship agreement between the sponsor and the association which is sent for signature by both parties.

Step 6

Invoice

After the agreements are signed, we send an invoice to the sponsor for payment of the agreed amount.

Step 7

Payment

After the invoice is paid, we complete the transaction and send the sponsorship to the selected association.

Step 8

Accounting

After the sponsorship has been carried out, the association is obliged to report the use of the funds as agreed (with invoices, receipts, pictures) within 6 months and we report this information to the sponsor.

Our Packages

Bronze

SEK 15,000

(Exclusive moms)

What are the benefits?

  • The association/team in the Bronze Package undertakes to promote the sponsor on the team's social media accounts, website and e-mail, as well as to send a "thank you photo" from the team that the sponsor can use on its social media, website, in stores and in other relevant marketing.
Silver

SEK 30,000

(Exclusive moms)

What are the benefits?

  • Our Silver package offers everything found in our Bronze package.
  • The team undertakes to create a well-made thank you video, where the team members express their gratitude to you as a sponsor.
  • In addition, we offer a customized and professional UI/UX design for a dedicated sponsor page on your website, perfectly adapted to your brand.
Gold

SEK 50,000

(Exclusive moms)

What are the benefits?

  • Our Gold package offers everything that is in our Bronze and Silver packages, as well as the exclusive opportunity to have your logo printed on the team's match kit.
Flexible

Custom Price

(Excluding VAT)

Our Flexible Package gives companies and sponsors the opportunity to tailor their effort to local teams and associations according to their unique wishes and goals. With a minimum amount of SEK 15,000 excluding VAT, this money can be used for various purposes as desired by the sponsor.

To prepare a detailed quotation and proposal, please contact us via our contact form and specify your wishes. Our team at SponsorPortalen will work with you to develop a solution that best meets your needs and expectations.

Bronze Package Details

  • Cost Breakdown

    Our Silver package is an excellent choice for companies that want to make a significant contribution to local teams and associations. With a price of SEK 30,000 excluding VAT, these funds can be used to pay registration fees for the cup, cup travel with hotel accommodation, travel costs, purchase of food and drink for sale at matches and cups (which gives the opportunity to generate additional income), purchase of necessary equipment, and others.

  • Benefits

    Our Silver package offers everything found in our Bronze package, with the added value of a personal and professional touch. The team undertakes to create a well-made thank you video, where the team members express their gratitude to you as a sponsor. In addition, we offer a customized and professional UI/UX design for a dedicated sponsor page on your website, perfectly adapted to your brand. This gives you an easy opportunity to show your commitment and support to the youth team on your own website.

  • Impact of Sponsorship

    Of the total amount of SEK 30,000, SEK 24,000 will go to the team, while the remaining SEK 6,000 will be used to cover administrative costs on the SponsorPortal, including costs for professional UI/UX design and customization of the sponsor page in accordance with your company's website and branding, video and image editing, as well as professional support and expertise in matching teams with appropriate sponsors based on their business values ​​and goals. This also includes continuous monitoring and management of communications, accountability and compliance to ensure a smooth and successful partnership for all involved.

Connecting Companies and Youth Sport Talents

Connection between companies

The Sponsor Portal functions as a connection between companies/sponsors who wish to support children's and youth sports through financial contributions and associations/teams seeking support for their activities.

Free of Charge

The Sponsor Portal is only intended for associations/teams in children's and youth sports. The association/team can register an interest in sponsorship through the form completely free of charge.

Register your team for the chance to be sponsored

Humanitarian Fund

The sponsor portal has its own humanitarian fund with the aim of supporting and helping children's athletes. To contribute to this fund we set aside 2% of every invoice and we encourage our sponsors to also contribute any amount to help reach our goal.

Opportunity Exists To Apply For Money For

Membership

Payment for membership and training fees to the association.

Equipment

To purchase necessary equipment, such as shoes and training clothes.

Training camp & Cups

To pay for cup trips and training camps.

"If you want your children to continue to practice football, it is important that the parents stand up as coaches and team managers," the association announced. They offered to help with materials, advice and training.

We didn't know what this would lead to at the time, but we decided to give it a try. We would still drive the boy to practice and wait for him until it was done. Then we might as well bring some balls and cones and organize some games and exercises. We would see if the boy was interested.
